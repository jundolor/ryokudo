About Ryokudo

JAC Co. Ltd. is a waste treatment company located in Miyagi Prefecture, with a proven technology for sorting the of recycling waste, resource recycling and energy regeneration. The company processes trash coming from a minimum of five cities per plant location. It has a proven track record of treatment of rubble that was generated after the Great East Japan Earthquake. It has also been tapped by JICA to undertake an eco-city project in Hai-phong City in Vietnam, having the 3rd largest population in the country with a pressing household and industrial waste problem.

JAC Co. Ltd. is represented in the Philippines by Ryokudo Eco Services and Trading Corporation.
