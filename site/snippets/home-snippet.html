Next Generation Technology

Refuse Plastic Fuel or RPF is the latest fueling technology, manufactured through the following A to C process.

  1. All combustible non‐industrial waste including kitchen waste are carbonized to get char
  2. The inappropriate portion are removed from the char. These are rinsed and dried for dechlorination
  3. Crushed waste plastic are mixed into the char to adjust calorific values and to form pellets
